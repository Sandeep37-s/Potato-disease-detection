About This Project

LeafLens is an intelligent tool designed to assist gardeners, farmers, and plant enthusiasts in identifying common plant diseases quickly and accurately. By leveraging the power of deep learning, this application can analyze an image of a plant leaf and predict whether it is healthy or affected by diseases like Early Blight or Late Blight.

How It Works

The core of this application is a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model trained on a large dataset of plant leaf images. When you upload an image, it is preprocessed and fed into the model, which then outputs a prediction along with a confidence score.

  • Upload: You provide an image of a plant leaf.
  • Analysis: The image is resized and analyzed by our Keras/TensorFlow model.
  • Result: The system provides a prediction, a confidence level, and helpful information about the detected condition.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make plant care more accessible and data-driven. Early disease detection is crucial for preventing crop loss and ensuring healthy plant growth. We hope this tool empowers users to take timely and informed action to protect their plants.
